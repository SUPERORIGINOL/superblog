Fortunately, I think this is retarded.

It's too easy to get caught up in what's honestly a "trend" of hating AI. And Its even easier to hate it just because. Personally, there is nothing I hate more than baseless discrimination. Hate is a powerful feeling, and can do big things. Therefore, it should be used in a way that promotes a positive change in the world. As it is all i have been talking about, I'm sure you are asking: "but Eli, what are some reasons to hate AI?" and oh boy, I am glad you asked.

First and foremost in my eyes is the corporatization of your humanity. Sam Altman, a conman who is pushing for the expansion of AI, recently spoke of how the technology will be simply a utility, mentioned in the same sentence of water and electricity. Pay by the thought, if you will. As Rene Descartes said, "I think, therefore I am"; Our conscious thought is what makes us truly human. As soon as your own thoughts get outsourced, what are you? A lack of agency breeds complacency. Complacent people are easy to control. And when you have all the money in the world, What is left? All the power. Power to the people, Am I right?
